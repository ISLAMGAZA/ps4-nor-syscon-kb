# Contributor Code of Conduct

We want this community to be welcoming, helpful, and focused on repairing PS4
consoles safely.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and patient, especially with newcomers to console repair.
- Share knowledge openly; the whole point of this project is a **shared** corpus.
- Respect privacy — never post serials, MAC addresses, or board IDs.
- Keep discussions on-topic and constructive.

## Unacceptable behaviour
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Posting identifying console data.
- Sabotaging, spoofing, or submitting fake/garbage corpus reports.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or block contributors who violate
this conduct. For concerns, contact the maintainer via a private security
advisory.

This project follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.
